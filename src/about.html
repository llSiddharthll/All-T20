About Premier Cricket League

Discover the story behind Asia's most prestigious cricket tournament and the vision that drives us forward.

Our Mission

To elevate cricket in Asia by creating a world-class platform that showcases talent, promotes sportsmanship, and unites fans through the spirit of competition.

  • Nurture emerging cricket talent
  • Promote cricket excellence
  • Unite cricket enthusiasts

Our Vision

To be the most competitive and respected cricket league in Asia, setting new standards in sports entertainment and professional cricket management.

  • Global recognition
  • Innovation in cricket
  • Community development
